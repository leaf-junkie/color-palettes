Palette 1: Emphasis on Enpira

A monochromatic palette (Enpira blue) with complimentary accent colors

Palette 2: Teal with Accent

Similar to palette 1, with teal instead of blue.

This kind of color scheme would be especially good for the load compare tab, with week days being monochromatic, weekends being complimentary to week days, and holidays being the accent color.

Palette 3: Durham-centric

A bold color palette that offers more contrast. Based on Durham's official color scheme.

This is one way we could customize the user's dashboard. It's a fun idea but perhaps not the most sustainable over time.

Palette 4: Sunset

A beautiful, natural gradient.

Palette 5: Forest

A simple and pleasant analogous gradient from dark blue to light yellow-green.

I like this mix of colors for their psychology. Blue: professional, trustworthy, dependable. Green: environment, health, savings. Yellow: energetic, optimism.
